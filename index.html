🚨 Critical Drop-Off Identified

The Browse → Checkout transition loses 2,500 users — the single largest drop in the entire funnel. Users are engaging with products but failing to convert intent into action.

62.5%of browsers never reach checkout

Even a 10% recovery = +250 additional purchases per 10K visitors.

02 — Root Cause Analysis

WHY USERS DON'T CHECKOUT

HIGH
🐌

Slow Page Load

Pages average 4.8s load time. Every 1s delay reduces conversion ~7%. Mobile users on 4G see 7s+ loads.

HIGH
💸

Confusing Pricing

Taxes revealed at checkout. Users see "$89" then "$127 total" — creating sticker shock and abandonment.

MED
📣

Weak CTA Placement

"Add to Cart" sits below the fold on mobile. 68% of traffic is mobile. Users scroll away before seeing it.

MED
🔀

Too Many Steps

7-step checkout flow vs. 3–4 step industry benchmark. Each extra step reduces completion ~10%.

LOW
🏅

No Social Proof

Product pages lack reviews at the point of decision. No trust signal before committing to checkout.

LOW
⏱

No Urgency Signal

Availability not communicated. Users feel no incentive to convert now vs. "I'll come back later."

03 — Recommended Solutions

THE FIX PLAYBOOK

01

Sticky CTA on Mobile Product Pages

Pin "Reserve Now" button to bottom of viewport on scroll. Eliminates the need to hunt for the button on mobile.

+12% CTR Est.
02

Transparent All-Inclusive Pricing

Show final price (taxes + fees) on product card. Eliminates checkout shock. Add "Price Guarantee" badge for trust.

−18% Abandon Est.
03

Streamline Checkout: 7 Steps → 3

Merge date/guest selection into Step 1. Auto-fill returning user info. Guest checkout removes account friction.

+25% Completion Est.
04

Inline Social Proof at Decision Point

Show "4.8★ (2,341 reviews)" directly below ticket title. Increases confidence before CTA click.

+8% CVR Est.

02 — Key Behavioral Insights

What the Data Revealed

📱

Mobile Users Drop Off Faster

Mobile users (68% of traffic) have a 74% bounce rate vs. 41% on desktop. Root cause: form fields too small, 4+ taps to checkout, slow image loading on mobile networks.

74%
mobile bounce rate
🔁

Returning Users Convert 3× More

Users on their 2nd+ visit convert at 18.4% vs. 6.2% for first-timers. They spend 2.3× longer on product pages and reach checkout at 2× the rate.

3×
return visitor CVR vs new
🌙

Evening Sessions Have Highest Intent

7–10PM sessions show 2.1× higher checkout completion. Users browse leisurely. Opportunity: time-sensitive offers during this window.

2.1×
evening session lift
🔍

Search Traffic Converts Better

Organic search converts at 8.9% vs. 3.2% from social ads. Search users arrive with higher intent. Recommendation: shift 30% of social spend to SEO.

8.9%
organic search CVR
