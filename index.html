02 — Key Behavioral Insights

What the Data
Revealed

📱

Mobile Users Drop Off Faster

Mobile users (68% of traffic) have a 74% bounce rate vs. 41% on desktop. Root cause: form fields too small, 4+ taps to reach checkout, slow image loading on mobile networks.

74%
mobile bounce rate
🔁

Returning Users Convert 3× More

Users on their 2nd+ visit convert at 18.4% vs. 6.2% for first-timers. They spend 2.3× longer on product pages and reach checkout at 2× the rate.

3×
return visitor CVR vs new
🌙

Evening Sessions Have Highest Intent

7–10PM sessions show 2.1× higher checkout completion. Users are browsing leisurely, not commuting. Opportunity: time-sensitive offers during this window.

2.1×
evening session conversion lift
🔍

Search Traffic Converts Better Than Social

Organic search visitors convert at 8.9% vs. 3.2% from social ads. Search users arrive with higher intent. Budget reallocation recommendation: shift 30% of social spend to SEO.

8.9%
organic search CVR

Engine

PERSONALIZATION RULES — LIVE ENGINE
NEW USER
visit=1
Show: "First Visit? Here's 10% Off Your First Booking"
Reduce first-visit friction. Incentive converts new users who are still in consideration phase.
+22% CVR
RETURNING
visit>1
no purchase
Show: Previously Viewed Items + "Only 3 Rooms Left" Scarcity Signal
High-intent returners need urgency, not discovery. Saved preferences reduce re-research time to zero.
+41% CVR
MOBILE
device=phone
Show: Simplified 1-screen form + Tap-to-call option
Eliminates multi-step friction. Phone option serves users who prefer human confirmation for high-value purchases.
+34% form complete
HIGH INTENT
session>8min
Show: Live chat prompt: "Have questions? Chat with our team"
Long sessions signal research mode. Live chat converts 58% of 8min+ sessions that haven't initiated checkout.
+58% chat CVR
